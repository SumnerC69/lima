About

Liam Butterworth is a talented musician and singer from Kirkland, Washington, bringing authentic emotion and craftsmanship to every performance.

A graduate of Juanita High School (Class of 2023), Liam's journey includes not only music but also athletics, having played varsity football for the Ravens. This diverse background informs his artistic perspective and work ethic.

With releases like "Kirkland Cowboy" and his festive "Butter Christmas" series, Liam combines thoughtful songwriting with compelling vocal performances that reflect his Pacific Northwest roots and unique personality.
